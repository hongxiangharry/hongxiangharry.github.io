Hongxiang (Harry) Lin

Email / Google Scholar / Github

I am now a Research Associate in the Centre for Medical Image Computing, Department of Computer Science at University College London.

My current research interests are Super-Resolved Deep Learning and Inverse Problems on Medical Imaging (Magnetic Resonance Imaging/Ultrasound Computed Tomography/Photoacoustic Imaging).

Previously, I was a Project Researcher affiliated to both Fluids Engineering Laboratory and Medical Equipment Engineering Laboratory at the University of Tokyo. I was awarded my PhD from Department of Mechanical Engineering, the University of Tokyo (2017). I obtained my MSc degree in mathematics at Fudan University (2014) and my BSc degree in mathematics at Xiamen University (2011).
